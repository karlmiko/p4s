Our story

The idea of using driveways and private spaces for student parking was born in the back of Marianopolis' computer lab. That's where Seb and Karl, our two founders, met up to study during the day. After successfully presenting their prototype at Mari's annual science fair and even being presented with their very first investment opportunity, the two partners decided to take their idea to the next level. A few months later, Parking4Students.com was launched!

Our mission

Parking4Students.com's mission is to provide low-cost, reliable parking to CEGEP and college students accross Montreal. By doing so, we want to maximize the utility of urban space and bring people to collaborate more together, from students to schools to homeowners. We are strong believers of the sharing economy and we think that by helping each other out, we can make the world a better place, one parking space at a time.
